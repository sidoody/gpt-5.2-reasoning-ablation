# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

This project aims to provide a respectful, constructive, and harassment-free environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional in discussion and review.
- Give feedback on ideas and code, not people.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions before escalating.
- Keep criticism specific, actionable, and civil.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Threats, intimidation, or sustained disruptive behavior.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or block participation for behavior that violates this code.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, open a private channel with the maintainer rather than a public issue.
